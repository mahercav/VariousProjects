#WorldCupPredictions

In this repository, I will analyze the World Cup data from 1970 to 2022 from Kaggle

 (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/abecklas/fifa-world-cup/data)

My goals for the project are as follows:

1. Can outcomes be predicted by data from previous World Cups?
2. Do yellow and red cards have a significant impact on win percentage?
3. How does a teams first half performance predict their chances of winning?

In this project, I investigate the data using a correlation matrix and visualization, I engineer important features, clean data, and build and deploy various models.

This project is in progress.
